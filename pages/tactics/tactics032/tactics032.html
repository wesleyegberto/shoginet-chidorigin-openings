Side Pawn Picker (Yokofudori) Strategy [2]

　

Within the Side Pawn Picker (Yokofudori) family, Gote playing ▽２三歩 is a variation that could be called a classic joseki (established theory). While it is more common nowadays to see ▽８六歩 played, this classic sequence is absolutely essential knowledge for understanding the latest modern variations.

Figure 1 shows the position after ▲１六歩 was played from Figure 3 of the previous chapter. While there are various moves available following Gote's ▽３三銀, ▲１六歩 is currently considered the best. The objective of this move is to "kill" the Bishop by following up with ▲３五金.

First, if Gote plays ▽２四歩 to create a path for the Bishop to retreat to ３四 or １四, Sente effectively responds with ▲３一金, targeting the Knight at ２一. The only way to defend is with ▽２二飛, but then Sente drops ▲２三歩 into the vacated space. After ▽同飛, Sente drops ▲３二角, resulting in an advantage for Sente.

Next, if Gote plays ▽４四歩 to create an escape route on that side, Sente immediately drops ▲６五角. Gote has no choice but to defend this Knight-targeting move with ▽３一飛. Defending with ▽２二飛 instead would lead to trouble after Sente's ▲３一金 drop. By utilizing pieces in the home camp with ▲３六歩, ▲３七桂, and ▲４六歩, Gote finds it difficult to effectively use their two Rooks. In Figure 3, the situation is favorable for Sente.

While there are further variations within these two examples and other moves available, the difficulty in handling the Bishop at ２五 makes Figure 1 a position full of hardships for Gote. Even for amateur dan-ranked players, playing the Gote side would be quite a challenge.

Furthermore, since it has become common to play ▲３六飛 in response to Gote's 16th move ▽２五角, actual game examples of Gote playing ▽２三歩 after ▲２四同飛 and allowing Sente to take the side pawn have decreased sharply. Consequently, numerous strategies have been born to counter Sente's Side Pawn Picker.
