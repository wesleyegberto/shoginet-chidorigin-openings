Double Wing Attack - Gote's 16th-move Choice

In the previous chapter, we learned why it is safer for Gote to respond to ▲1六歩 with ▽1四歩. Following that, after Sente's 15th move ▲3八銀, the Hineri-bisha commentary shows Gote playing ▽6四歩. While other moves could be considered here, it is actually difficult for Gote to choose anything other than ▽6四歩 for their 16th move. I will provide a brief explanation as to why.

In this position, where only Sente has exchanged the Rook-file Pawn, a Bougin (Climbing Silver) attack by Gote is not advantageous. By playing ▲7六歩 followed by ▲7七角, Sente prevents Gote from exchanging their Rook-file Pawn. Sente’s maneuver of rising with ▲8八銀 against ▽7四歩 is an effective intercepting formation that eliminates any worry of Gote breaking through the 8th file. When Gote advances with ▽7五銀, Sente launches an edge attack starting with ▲2五飛 and ▲1五歩, leading to a superior position for Sente in "Figure 2."

If Gote does not take ▲1四歩 with ▽同香, Sente can tighten the edge with ▲1五飛 followed by ▽1二歩, which still results in a superior game for Sente.

As for other moves in "Figure 1," ▽4二銀 is problematic as it allows Sente to counter with their own Climbing Silver via ▲2七銀. Additionally, ▽5四歩 often becomes a weakness later if Sente plays ▲9七角 during a Hineri-bisha transition, or it could lead to a Jyuuji-bisya (Cross-Rook) tactic starting with ▲2四歩. Playing ▽4二玉 prevents Gote from forming the Kanemino (Gold Mino) castle discussed in Chapter 28. So, what about ▽4一玉?

The sequence leading to "Figure 3" is just one example, but it demonstrates that if Sente switches to a Koshikake-gin (Reclining Silver) with ▲5六銀, Gote's King at ▽4一玉 becomes poorly positioned. Against this type of Reclining Silver formation, the King is better placed at either 4-2 or 5-2 for better balance.

While Gote has other options, ▽6四歩 is the move with the least downside, as it allows Gote to adapt to whatever tactical formation Sente chooses. For these reasons, in this Aigakari style—including variations that lead to Hineri-bisha—moves up to Gote's 16th move (▽6四歩) are considered almost inevitable.

Note that for Sente's 13th move, playing ▲5八玉 is an alternative to ▲1六歩. However, choosing ▲5八玉 eliminates the option to transition into Hineri-bisha later. Since ▲5八玉 is rarely seen in professional games, ▲1六歩 remains the standard choice.
