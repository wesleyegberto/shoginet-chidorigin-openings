Side Pawn Picker - ▽3八 Pawn Strategy [1]

　

The ▽４五角 Opening explained in the previous chapters has currently reached the conclusion that Sente is generally at an advantage, and the strategy has largely disappeared from the professional Shogi world. However, there is actually another powerful variation that shares similar goals to the ▽４五角 strategy but is considered effective.

From the basic Yokofudori (Side Pawn Picker) position starting with ▽８六歩, the sequence is the same as the ▽４五角 Opening up until the Bishop exchange. However, instead of playing at ２八, Gote drops ▽３八歩 at this square.

Moves from Figure 1 to Figure 2:

  • ▲同　金
  • ▽４五角
  • ▲２四飛
  • ▽６七角成
  • ▲同　金
  • ▽８八飛成

In response to ▽３八歩, Sente has the options of ▲同金, ▲同銀, or retreating with ▲２八銀. If Sente plays ▲同金, Gote follows with the Bishop exchange and the ▽４五角 drop. If the game then proceeds similarly to the ▽４五角 Opening variations discussed in chapter 34 up to Figure 2, Gote creates a tsumero (threatmate) starting with ▽５八銀. Consequently, Sente cannot play ▲２一飛成, leaving Gote in a winning position. The ▲３八金 formation results in a "wall" shape that blocks the King's escape route; this bad shape creates a significant difference in the outcome.

Retreating with ▲２八銀 also leads to trouble. Following the sequence from ▽２七歩 up to Figure 3, the result is the same as when Sente plays ▲同金: the ▽８八飛成 move becomes a tsumero (threatmate), leaving Sente at a disadvantage.

If Gote plays ▽４五角 after ▲同銀, the position of the Silver at ３八 actually provides better coordination than the variation where the Silver is drawn to ２八. In that case, contrary to Figure 2 and Figure 3, Sente would have the advantage. Therefore, Gote instead plays ▽４四角, aiming for the sequence ▽８八角成 ▲同金 ▽同飛成. Sente plays ▲８七歩 to prevent this, and the game proceeds naturally with ▽７六飛 ▲７七銀 ▽７四飛. However, in reality, Gote already holds a superior position by Figure 4.
