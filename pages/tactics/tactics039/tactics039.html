While Figure 2 is considered a winning position for Sente, beginners might find it difficult to see how Sente has the advantage even at this stage. In Figure 1, Sente also has other defensive options such as ▲７七歩 or ▲７七桂, but both lead to extremely complex variations.

To be honest, I do not intend to explain the complex Ai-yoko-fudori (Double Side Pawn Picker) joseki in this "Strategy Encyclopedia." This opening has a vast number of variations, and every single one of them is so fierce that a single mistake will send you plunging into the abyss. Furthermore, anyone who initiates this strategy likely has studied the joseki extensively; unless you have also researched the variations deeply, you may be defeated without even a chance to put up a fight.

Therefore, in this course, I would like to teach a recommended countermeasure for beginners. We will cover that explanation in the next chapter.
