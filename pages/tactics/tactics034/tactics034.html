The direct aim of ▽４五角 is the sequence ▽６七角成 followed by ▽８八飛成. However, if Gote plays ▽６七角成 immediately when Sente plays ▲２四飛, Sente's counter-drop of ▲４五角 in Figure 3 is a brilliant offensive and defensive move. This causes Gote's attack to stall, resulting in a winning position for Sente. Furthermore, because Sente is threatening with ▲２一飛成, Gote cannot afford to take their time. Therefore, Gote must first play ▽２三歩 in response to ▲２四飛.

In this situation, if the exchange of ▽２八歩 ▲同銀 had not taken place, Sente could retreat with ▲２八飛. This would allow the Rook's horizontal power to reach ８八, effectively neutralizing the ▽６七角成 threat.
