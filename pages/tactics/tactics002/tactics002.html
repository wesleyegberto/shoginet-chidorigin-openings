Yagura Opening

Among games where both players compete using Ibisha (Static Rook), the strategy that begins with Sente opening the Bishop's diagonal with ▲7六歩 is known as Yagura. An opening that starts this way is called a "Yagura-style" setup.

<>On the 5th move, ▲7七銀 is sometimes played instead of ▲6六歩. In the past, ▲7七銀 was more common and almost guaranteed that Sente would commit to the Yagura formation. However, ▲6六歩 is played more frequently nowadays. While this move leaves open the possibility of Sente transitioning into other strategies, if they intend to play Static Rook, it is standard for the game to proceed into a Yagura formation from here.

The castle shown in "Figure 2" is called the Yagura Castle. When both players aim for this specific defensive formation, the game is referred to as Ai-Yagura (Double Yagura). While there are other variations of Yagura, this "Kin-Yagura" (Gold Yagura) is the most common and standard form.
